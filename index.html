About Gingersnips

Coleraine's Award-Winning Boutique Salon

Founded by Lesley Ann McNeill in 1992, Gingersnips has grown from a neighbourhood hairdresser into one of Coleraine's most loved and trusted salons. Over three decades of dedication to the craft means you're in hands that truly know hair.

We're a proud unisex salon — welcoming men, women, and children of all ages. Whether you're after a fresh cut, a bold new colour, or simply a wash and blow dry to feel your best, our team of four fully qualified stylists is here for you.

Our Services

Everything Your Hair Needs, Under One Roof

From a quick trim to a complete colour transformation — our experienced team has you covered.

Cuts & Styling

Precision cuts tailored to your face shape, lifestyle, and personality. From classic trims to bold new looks — for men, women, and children.

Colouring

Full colour, highlights, balayage, and ombre using Matrix's low-ammonia, high-shine colour system. Vibrant results that last and keep your hair healthy.

Blow Dry & Finish

Walk out looking and feeling incredible with a professional blow dry. Perfect before a night out, special event, or just because you deserve it.

Treatments

Deep conditioning, keratin smoothing, and repair treatments using Moroccan Oil and Matrix Biolage. Restore strength, shine, and softness to damaged hair.

Perms

Body, volume, and texture — our modern perm techniques give you bouncy, natural-looking curls and waves that last for months.

Occasion Styling

Weddings, proms, parties — look absolutely stunning for your big day. Updos, curls, braids, and everything in between.
